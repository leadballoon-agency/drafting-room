The Drafting Room Method

Six steps to sartorial perfection

01

Consultation

Your vision meets our expertise in a relaxed, unhurried consultation. Explore our curated selection of over 5,000 fabrics while we discuss your lifestyle, preferences, and sartorial ambitions.

1.5 hours | Complimentary whisky | Style assessment
02

Measurement & Design

Precision mapping of your form using 28 points of measurement. We create your unique pattern - a blueprint that captures not just your dimensions, but your posture and movement.

28 measurements | Digital pattern creation | Posture analysis
03

Pattern Drafting

Your blueprint comes to life as our master cutters translate measurements into a unique paper pattern. This becomes your permanent template, archived for future commissions.

Master cutting | Pattern archival | Quality assurance
04

Construction

Over 6-8 weeks, your suit passes through the hands of specialized craftsmen. Each contributing their expertise - from floating the canvas to hand-stitching the buttonholes.

150+ hours | Hand-padded lapels | 3,000+ stitches
05

Fitting & Refinement

The moment of truth. We conduct a comprehensive fitting, making minute adjustments to ensure the suit moves with you, breathes with you, and feels like a second skin.

Precision adjustments | Balance perfection | Final approval
06

Delivery

Your blueprint realized. We present your completed suit with care instructions and our commitment to lifetime pattern storage. First-year adjustments are complimentary.

Final presentation | Care guide | Lifetime relationship
